The Best Movies According to Nicole

My top 3 movies of all time.

Phantom of the Opera

A disfigured musical genius who lives in the Paris Opera House becomes obsessed with a young soprano. However, love turns into a murderous rage when his young protege falls in love with another.

Blade Runner

Rick Deckard, an ex-policeman, becomes a special agent with a mission to exterminate a group of violent androids. As he starts getting deeper into his mission, he questions his own identity.

Spirited Away

Ten-year-old Chihiro and her parents end up at an abandoned amusement park inhabited by supernatural beings. Soon, she learns that she must work to free her parents who have been turned into pigs.
